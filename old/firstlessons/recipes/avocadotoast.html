Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • Fresh avocados 2
  • Multigrain bread
  • Herbs
  • Olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

How to prepare

  1. Prepare avocado paste. Use avocados and just peal it off and smesh with a fork.
  2. Slice a breade to 1-1,5 cm pieces.
  3. Make the toasts from bread peaces.
  4. Smeal avocado paste for toasts. Add herbs, olive oil, salt and pepper.
  5. Enjoy your meal!
